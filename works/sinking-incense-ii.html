Program Notes

In the realms of time and space, sound and scent share similar patterns and trajectories. Sinking Incense Ⅱ is not merely a sonic portrayal of a fragrant object, but a piece of agarwood itself, embedded in time. From the very first moment, it scents, and it sounds.
