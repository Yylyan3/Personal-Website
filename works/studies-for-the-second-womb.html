Program Notes

This project stems from a profound reflection on a universal auditory experience shared by humanity: the sounds perceived by a fetus within its maternal womb. The title the second womb refers both to that original listening space and to a future, imagined artificial womb—a concept that fuels my vision of humanity's prospective trajectory.
